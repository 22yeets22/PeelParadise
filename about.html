Commonly Asked Questions

What is our business concept?

The concept the we envision for our business is an amazing banana shop with a wide range of products, all ethically, and sustainably sourced.

When/How was our business established?

Peel Paradise was established in 2015 by three good friends, and since then, we have grown a lot. Now, we have 5 banana factories in Colombia, and 100's of shops across the United States. Recently, we have began expanding our business to Canada.

What is our mission and product/service?

Our mission is to provide quality, pesticide free, ethically sourced bananas straight from our factories in Colombia to your table at a price that ant family can afford.

Where is our business located?

Peel Paradise's many factories are located in the heart of rural Colombia. That is where the real magic happens. We have numerous (hundreds of) locations in the United States, and we are now expanding our business to Canada.
